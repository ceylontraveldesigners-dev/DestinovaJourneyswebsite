Day 01 Sigiriya · Arrival

Arrival — A Spectacular Entry by Air

Upon arrival in Sri Lanka, you are warmly welcomed by a representative of Destinova Journeys and escorted directly to the helicopter terminal for a spectacular aerial transfer to Sigiriya (approx. 50 minutes). From above, the island's extraordinary tapestry unfolds — the glittering coastline gives way to paddy fields, jungle canopy, and the ancient cultural triangle stretching below.

Touch down at the private helipad of Jetwing Vil Uyana, where your personal chauffeur and the property team await. Settle into your Forest Dwelling — an elegant sanctuary set within 14 acres of reconstructed wetland wilderness, where nature has been coaxed back to extraordinary abundance. The rest of the evening is yours to unwind, absorb the remarkable surroundings, and ease gently into the island's unhurried rhythm.

Days 02 — 03 Sigiriya · Ancient Kingdom

Village Life, Wilderness & Lost Kingdoms

Day 2 is a deep immersion in authentic rural life. Walk through a traditional Sri Lankan village and observe age-old farming techniques — fields protected from elephants by ingenious methods, farmers spending nights in elevated treehouses under the stars. Glide across a serene lake by canoe before indulging in a home-cooked Sri Lankan lunch prepared over a traditional wood-fire kitchen. In the evening, venture into the surrounding wilderness for a curated nocturnal experience: a rare loris watching tour and guided walk through the forest as it comes alive after dark.

On Day 3, journey to Polonnaruwa — a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka's most remarkably preserved medieval capitals. Discover the towering Gal Vihara rock sculptures, the vast Parakrama Samudra reservoir, and a complex of palaces, temples, and monasteries that speak of a civilisation at the very height of its power. Your expert guide brings the ancient stones to life with stories of kings, conquests, and centuries of devotion.

Days 04 — 05 Hatton · Hill Country

Tea, Mist & the Art of the Highland Life

On Day 4, a breathtaking helicopter journey to Hatton (approx. 50 minutes) reveals one of Sri Lanka's most dramatic landscape transitions — from the warm, ancient plains of the cultural triangle to the cool, mist-veiled emerald highlands of tea country. Your chauffeured transfer brings you to the legendary Ceylon Tea Trails, where a private colonial-era bungalow nestled amidst rolling plantations becomes your home for the next two nights. The all-inclusive experience — fine wines, curated dining, and the bungalow's entire team entirely at your disposal — is the very definition of unhurried privilege.

Day 5 is dedicated to the living heritage of Ceylon tea. Visit a working plantation and factory to trace the extraordinary journey from freshly plucked leaf to the perfectly brewed cup — meeting the tea pluckers, learning to distinguish grades, and gaining an intimate appreciation of the craft that made Ceylon tea synonymous with quality the world over. The afternoon belongs entirely to you: a stroll along the reservoir, high tea on the lawn, or simply watching the light fade over the hills from your veranda.

Days 06 — 07 Yala · Southern Wilderness

Into the Wild — The Kingdom of the Leopard

Bid farewell to the highlands and take a scenic helicopter journey south to Yala, where the landscape transitions dramatically from cool, mist-wrapped tea country to the raw, sun-drenched wilderness of Sri Lanka's most celebrated national park. Check into the extraordinary Uga Chena Huts — an intimate collection of private cabins, each with its own plunge pool, set within the very edge of the park. The afternoon brings your first game drive through Yala's varied ecosystems, home to the world's highest density of leopards as well as elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and a dazzling array of birdlife.

Day 7 begins before dawn. Rise for a sunrise game drive through Yala's most active hours — when the park's extraordinary cast of characters moves freely across the landscape in the cool morning light. Return to Chena Huts for a leisurely breakfast and the rest of the day at pure leisure: linger by your private pool, observe the extraordinary bird life from your cabin deck, or simply absorb the rare sense of wilderness that surrounds you on every side.

Days 08 — 09 Tangalle · Southern Coast

Tangalle — The Quiet Art of Doing Nothing

After breakfast, a scenic coastal drive along Sri Lanka's southern shore brings you to the exquisite Amanwella — a sanctuary of absolute privacy perched above one of the island's most beautiful and unspoilt stretches of coastline. Your Ocean Hill Pool Suite commands sweeping views of the Indian Ocean, and the signature Aman sense of calm — that rare quality that transforms a beach into something closer to a state of mind — envelops you from the moment of arrival.

Two days here belong entirely to you. Unwind on Tangalle's pristine golden sands, far from the tourist trail. Surrender to the soothing rhythm of the ocean. Indulge in spa rituals designed to restore and replenish, snorkel in crystalline waters, or simply do nothing at all — with the particular confidence that nothing here could be more perfectly done. It is the ideal counterpoint to all that came before: a finale of pure, luminous stillness.
