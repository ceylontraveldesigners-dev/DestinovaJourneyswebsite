Day 02 Sigiriya

Village Immersion & Nature

Enjoy a leisurely day discovering authentic rural life. Walk through a traditional village and observe age-old farming techniques, where fields are protected from elephants and farmers spend nights in elevated treehouses. Glide across a serene lake by canoe before indulging in a home-cooked Sri Lankan lunch prepared in a rustic village setting. You may also take part in the cooking experience using traditional firewood methods. In the evening, explore the surrounding wilderness with curated nature experiences including loris watching and a guided nocturnal walk.

Highlights
◆Traditional village canoe experience
◆Home-cooked Sri Lankan cooking class
◆Evening loris watching & nocturnal walk

Day 03 Sigiriya

Polonnaruwa — The Ancient Capital

After breakfast, venture to Polonnaruwa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka's most remarkable ancient capitals. Discover well-preserved ruins, sacred temples, and impressive stone sculptures that reflect the grandeur of the island's medieval past. Your expert guide will illuminate the rich history and cultural significance of this once-great kingdom, offering a deeply personal connection to Sri Lanka's extraordinary civilisation.

Highlights
◆UNESCO World Heritage guided tour
◆Expert local historian accompanies you
◆Ancient stone sculptures & sacred temples
OvernightJetwing Vil Uyana
RoomForest Dwelling
BoardBed & Breakfast
Days 04 — 05 Hatton · Hill Country

Tea Trails — Highland Serenity

Depart by helicopter to Hatton, soaring over lush landscapes before descending into the emerald hills of Sri Lanka's legendary tea country. Your chauffeur transfers you to an elegant colonial bungalow nestled amidst rolling plantations. On Day 5, visit a working tea plantation and factory to witness the journey of Ceylon tea — from freshly plucked leaf to the perfect cup. Discover the craftsmanship behind one of the world's finest teas, before spending the remainder of the day at leisure, soaking in the tranquillity and breathtaking highland panoramas.

Highlights
◆Helicopter transfer over hill country
◆Working tea plantation & factory visit
◆Colonial bungalow — all inclusive
OvernightCeylon Tea Trails
RoomLuxury Bungalow
BoardAll Inclusive
Days 06 — 07 Yala National Park

Into the Wild — Yala Safari

Bid farewell to the hills and embark on a scenic helicopter journey to Yala, where landscapes transition dramatically from misty mountains to untamed wilderness. In the afternoon, your private jeep safari begins in Yala National Park — renowned for harbouring the world's highest density of leopards, alongside elephants, sloth bears, and extraordinary birdlife. Rise before dawn on Day 7 for another exhilarating game drive through Yala's varied ecosystems, encountering wildlife in their most unguarded, natural moments.

Highlights
◆Helicopter arrival into Yala wilderness
◆World's highest leopard density
◆Private game drives at dawn & dusk
OvernightUga Chena Huts
RoomCabin with Private Pool
BoardAll Inclusive
Days 08 — 09 Tangalle · Southern Coast

Amanwella — Coastal Seclusion

After breakfast, a scenic coastal drive leads you to the iconic Amanwella, perched above a private crescent beach on Sri Lanka's serene southern shore. Two days of pure, unhurried luxury await — unwind on pristine golden sands, surrender to the soothing rhythm of the Indian Ocean, and indulge in the signature Aman sense of calm. Your Ocean Hill Pool Suite offers sweeping sea views and absolute privacy, the perfect sanctuary to conclude your Sri Lankan journey.
