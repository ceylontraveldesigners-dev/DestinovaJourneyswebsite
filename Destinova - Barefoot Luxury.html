Days 01 — 02 Negombo

Arrival — Where the Ocean Welcomes You

Upon arrival in Sri Lanka, you are warmly welcomed by a representative of Destinova Journeys and escorted to the charming coastal town of Negombo. After your journey, unwind in the tranquil surroundings of Uga Riva, your boutique sanctuary by the sea. The rest of the day is yours to relax, recover, and ease into the island's gentle, unhurried rhythm.

On Day 2, discover Negombo at leisure — a town often called "Little Rome" for its abundance of historic churches, a legacy of Portuguese and Dutch colonial rule. Visit the vibrant fish market at dawn, observe traditional catamarans gliding along the lagoon, and stroll through streets rich in architecture and local life. The Negombo Lagoon, dotted with fishing boats and fringed by mangroves, offers a serene and quintessentially Sri Lankan afternoon.

Days 03 — 04 Anuradhapura
Day 05 Wilpattu

Sacred Ruins & The Wild North

On Day 3, a breathtaking helicopter journey (approx. 50 minutes) transports you to Anuradhapura, where you are met and transferred to the exquisite Uga Ulagalla — a private sanctuary set within 58 acres of verdant paddy fields and tropical gardens. The afternoon is yours to settle in, cycle through the estate, or simply absorb the extraordinary tranquillity.

Day 4 is devoted to the ancient city of Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site dating back over 2,000 years and among Asia's greatest archaeological wonders. Discover the towering Ruwanwelisaya and Jetavanaramaya stupas, ancient monastic complexes, and the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi — the oldest documented tree in the world, believed to be a cutting from the tree under which Buddha attained enlightenment.

On Day 5, a leisurely morning gives way to an exhilarating private jeep safari in Wilpattu National Park — Sri Lanka's largest and most pristine wildlife reserve. Wilpattu's extraordinary "villus" (natural lakes) and dense jungle create the perfect habitat for elusive leopards, sloth bears, wild elephants, and a spectacular array of birdlife. The park's exceptional quietude makes every sighting feel truly intimate.

Days 06 — 07 Hatton · Hill Country

Hatton — Tea, Mist & the Highlands

On Day 6, a breathtaking helicopter journey to Hatton (approx. 45 minutes) reveals one of the island's most dramatic landscape transitions — from the dry ancient plains of the north to the cool, emerald highlands of Sri Lanka's tea country. Fly over cascading waterfalls, rolling plantations, and mist-veiled ridges before descending into the hills. Check into the elegant Uga Halloowella, set amidst working tea estates with sweeping mountain views.

Day 7 is an immersion in Ceylon's legendary tea heritage. Visit a working plantation and factory where you will trace the journey from freshly plucked leaf to the perfectly brewed cup — meeting local tea pluckers, learning the art of grading, and gaining an intimate understanding of the craft that made Ceylon tea a byword for quality the world over. Return to Halloowella for a leisurely evening in the cool mountain air.

Days 08 — 10 Tangalle · Southern Coast

Tangalle — The Art of Doing Nothing

A final helicopter transfer (approx. 45 minutes) sweeps you from the cool highlands to the deep blue of the Indian Ocean, tracing the southern coastline as golden beaches and turquoise waters come into view. Check into the spectacular Uga Prāva — an intimate sanctuary perched above one of Sri Lanka's most beautiful and unspoilt stretches of coast.

Three days of pure, barefoot luxury await. Surrender to the rhythm of the ocean, sink into pristine sands, and indulge in spa rituals designed to restore and replenish. Tangalle's raw, unhurried beauty — far from the tourist trail — is the perfect finale to a journey through the soul of Sri Lanka. Days here belong entirely to you.
